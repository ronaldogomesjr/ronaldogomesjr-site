design

Design is understood as a practice of creating, mediating and organising learning experiences. More than an aesthetic or instrumental dimension, design involves pedagogical, technological, discursive and social choices that shape ways of teaching, learning, interacting and making meaning.

Perspective

At the intersection of language, technology and education, design helps us consider how materials, environments, media, tasks and digital resources can support more meaningful, critical and participatory learning experiences.
