language education

Language education is understood as a field of education, critical practice and knowledge production about language. More than developing isolated skills, it brings together reading, writing, speaking, listening and reflection to expand repertoires, participation, authorship and understanding across different contexts.

Perspective

Language is a social, historical and ethical practice. Language education creates conditions for each person to understand the world, express themselves, engage in dialogue and transform realities. This perspective values multiple voices, diversity, equity and the shared construction of meaning.
